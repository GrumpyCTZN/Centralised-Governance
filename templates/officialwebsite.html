Ministry of Home Affairs

The MoHA is responsible for administering, regulating, and implementing the laws and policies related to citizenship across the country.

Department of National ID and Civil Registration

The Department of National ID and Civil Registration (DoNIDCR) in Nepal is the primary government body responsible for managing, issuing, and maintaining the National Identity (NID) Card system

The Department of Transport Management (DOTM)

The Department of Transport Management (DoTM) in Nepal is the primary government body responsible for issuing, renewing, and managing all driving licenses, including handling the entire process from online applications, exams, biometrics, and printing smart cards.

The Department of Passports (DoP)

The Department of Passports (DoP) in Nepal, under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, manages the issuance of Nepali passports (including electronic passports or e-passports) to eligible citizens.
